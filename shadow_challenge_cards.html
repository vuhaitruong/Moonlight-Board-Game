● SHADOW CHALLENGE 01 / 36
The Stolen Idea
Credit for your work migrates to someone else
─── ✦ ───
The Scenario
In a team meeting, you propose a new approach to a persistent problem. There is a pause. The conversation moves on. Ten minutes later, a colleague repeats your idea almost word for word — and the room responds with enthusiasm. Your manager thanks them.
The Impact
Research shows this happens disproportionately to women, people of colour, and those with quieter communication styles. Each occurrence chips at credibility and confidence.
Discussion Prompt
"What would you do in this moment — and what would make it easier or harder to act?"
