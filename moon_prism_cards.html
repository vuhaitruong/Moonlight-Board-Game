The Feeling of Belonging
On what it means to truly be welcomed, not just tolerated
─── ✦ ───

"Describe a moment when you felt truly included — not just present, but genuinely welcome. What made that possible? Who made it possible?"

Facilitator Note
Invite people to be specific. General answers ("when I felt accepted") are less useful than concrete ones ("when she remembered my name the second time we met").
Deepening Question
"What specifically did someone do — or not do — that created that feeling? Could you replicate that for someone else this week?"

What You're Carrying Out
Closing reflection — to be used at the end of every session
─── ✦ ───

"As we close this session — what is one thing you are carrying out of this room that you were not carrying in? It could be a thought, a question, a feeling, or a commitment. Just one thing."

Facilitator Note
This is the standard closing prompt for every Moonlight Voices session. Go around the full circle. Do not allow discussion after each answer — just listen. End in silence for ten seconds before closing.
Facilitator Closing
"In the name of Love and Justice — thank you for being here, fully. What you shared today matters. Now go and do something with it."
